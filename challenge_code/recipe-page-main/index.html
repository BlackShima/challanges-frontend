Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy, quick dish—perfect any time. Add cheese, vegetables, or meats if desired.

Preparation time

  • Total: ~10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2–3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper (add 1 tbsp water or milk for fluffier texture).
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Pour eggs into the pan, tilting to spread evenly.
  4. When edges set but center is slightly runny, add fillings to one half.
  5. Fold the omelette and cook another minute, then serve hot.
